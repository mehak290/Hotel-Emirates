Our Mission

Our purpose is to provide every guest with a memorable, luxurious, and culturally rich experience. At Emirates Hotel, we aim to set new standards in hospitality by blending comfort, elegance, and tradition with outstanding service and sustainability.

Our History

Founded in 2005, Emirates Hotel began as a family-run vision rooted in the heart of Dubai. From a humble 20-room guesthouse, it has grown into a globally recognized symbol of Emirati hospitality, blending tradition with innovation while welcoming thousands of guests from all over the world.
